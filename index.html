Barteree Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Zero Tolerance Policy

Barteree strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We are committed to protecting children and supporting law enforcement.

Reporting Abuse

If you encounter any content or behavior related to CSAE, please report it immediately to support@barteree.com. We review all reports promptly and take appropriate action.

Cooperation with Authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement and relevant authorities to investigate and prevent CSAE. All reported cases are handled with the utmost seriousness and confidentiality.

Proactive Measures

We use automated tools and manual review to detect and remove CSAE content. Our team is trained to handle such cases sensitively and effectively. We regularly update our safety protocols to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Legal Compliance

Barteree complies with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child protection, including but not limited to COPPA and local laws. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of safety and responsibility.
